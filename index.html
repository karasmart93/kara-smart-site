The Optum Transformation

How a complete agile overhaul turned a drowning L&D team into a high-performing operation — in months, not years.

The Problem

Optum’s L&D team was drowning in ad-hoc requests, unclear intake processes, and constant context-switching. Projects routinely ran over budget. Designers were burning out. Stakeholders were frustrated by slow turnaround times and inconsistent quality.

The Approach

I designed and led a complete agile transformation — implementing Scrum ceremonies, rebuilding the intake system from scratch, creating transparent capacity planning tools, and coaching team members on sustainable workflows. Real change management with real buy-in.

The Outcome

Within months, chaos became clarity. Leadership got visibility. Designers got breathing room. Stakeholders got better, faster results. The team transformed from reactive firefighting to proactive, scalable delivery.
